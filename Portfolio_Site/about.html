about_Me

My name is Alastair and I'm originally from the UK but have lived in Brisbane, Australia for the past 4 years now.

Having completed a BA Hons of Music in the UK, I spent 15 years in a band, writing, recording and performing our own songs while holding down a variety of full-time jobs.

Since moving to Australia I've worked as an AV audio engineer for Microhire & BCEC in Brisbane, before joining Apple Retail on the Genius Bar in 2018.

I'm still a very creative person and love writing songs, record production, engineering and mixing - taking on projects when I have spare time.

My favourite coding language is JavaScript and I'm also very interested in blockchain technology.
